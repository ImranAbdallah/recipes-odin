Harees Recipe

Description

The Harees recipe is a traditional Arabic one pot dish made with wheat and chicken. You can even make this amazing recipe with boneless lamb too.

A traditional Middle-Eastern dish which is made in almost all the Arab and Muslim households, the Harees or harissa made daily during Ramadan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of whole wheat grains
  • 1 kg of chicken, deboned
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • salt to taste
  • first cold pressed virgin olive oil or melted butter

Steps

  1. Soak the wheat overnight
  2. In a very large pot, add the soaked wheat, chicken, cinnamon, salt and add enough water to bring to a boil
  3. Cook it untill the harees becomes a watery consistency. Remember to stir at the bottom every few minutes to prevent the bottom from getting burnt. This will take some time to get cooked.
  4. With a hand blender, blend it well, The consistency Should Not be like a fine paste and left little bit grainy
  5. Serve it with the melted butter or olive oil on top

Additional Notes

  • Harees Takes Time to Cook When cooked, it will be somewhat like on oats porridge.
  • You can also serve with or add any spices of your choice
